Discover Saratov, Russia

Saratov is one of the most beautiful cities, standing on the banks of the Volga River.
The city is located on the bank of the Volga River, in the center of the vast plain of European Russia.
The Saratov region is one of the most developed and densely populated areas of Russia.

Victory Park
Victory Park is on Sokolovaya Hill. It has a huge monument with cranes on its top dedicated to the memory of Saratov citizens who perished in the Great Patriotic War.
Opera Theatre and Teatralnaya Square
The historic and spiritual centre of this city. You can visit the square at any time, day and night, without worrying about opening hours and entrance tickets.
Opera Theatre is open only at a fixed time.
The Fine Arts Museum named after Radishchev
There is a world famous picture gallery (the Fine Arts Museum) in Saratov which was opened to the public on July 26, 1885. It is one of the largest picture galleries in Russia.
Named after Radishchev, the museum contains more than 15000 outstanding works of Russian and European artists.
Russian National Circus
The Russian National Circus was founded more than a century ago by the Nikitins Brothers. It was the first Russian permanent circus.
Now it is very popular among children and adults.
